Composite picture of a hairdresser-like woman who is jumping

Photoshop {{< figure src="/images/photos/photo06_firstview_en.png" alt="">}}

Prepare a cropped image

Prepare an image of a woman with a nice feeling that is jumping.

Crop the image

Using the "Pen tool" of Photoshop, we will trace around the image with a path.

Based on the path, create a selection and mask it.

Using the "borderline adjustment brush tool", the hair is masked so as not to feel uncomfortable with a good feeling.

Prepare an image for scissors

In order to synthesize scissors to a woman, prepare an image holding a scissors for a hairdresser.

Cut scissors

Using Pen tool, take a pass so that it feels nice.

Based on the path, create a selection and mask it.

Combine scissors

Once you cut out the scissors, it composes it so that there is no discomfort in the image of the girl.

Add shadows to scissors

As this is still a little uncomfortable, I will make a shadow of scissors.

Along the shadow of the scissors' original image, make a selection area with a nice touch and make a shadow.

With a gradation overlay, adjust it so that the upper one becomes slightly transparent.

Adjust the shadow of the scissors

Adjust the shadow of the scissors.

Adjust the opacity of the layer so that it feels nice.

Composite

Because it was able to clip and synthesize, I will synthesize it with a good feeling! (Actually adjusts tone curve, color balance, etc.)
